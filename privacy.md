## Data Collection

R3 does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All recorded steps and workflows remain entirely on your local machine within your browser's storage.

- No analytics or telemetry
- No user accounts or sign-ups
- No cloud servers or external network requests
- No cookies or tracking of any kind

## Permissions

R3 requests the following browser permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|---|---|
| **storage** | Save your recorded steps locally within the browser |
| **scripting** | Inject the recorder and player into web pages so R3 can capture and replay your actions |
| **tabs** | Navigate to URLs during playback and detect page loads |
| **host_permissions (all URLs)** | Allow recording and playback on any website you choose to use R3 on |

These permissions are never used to read, collect, or transmit your browsing data.

## Third Parties

R3 does not include any third-party libraries, SDKs, or services. There are no ads, no analytics providers, and no external dependencies.
